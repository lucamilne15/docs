<Note>
  This occurs when the applicant is not a permanent resident or citizen.
</Note>

## Platform Action

Always escalated to human for review.

## Example Situation

An applicant uploads a visa grant letter showing they hold a temporary subclass 482 visa.\
The system flags the non-PR status and escalates the application.\
Credit operations checks the Acceptable Visa List, confirms the 482 visa is eligible, and proceeds with further assessment.
