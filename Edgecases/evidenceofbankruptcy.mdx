---
title: "Edgecase: Evidence of Bankruptcy or Insolvency"
---

<Note>This occurs when the applicant’s credit report shows any record of bankruptcy, insolvency, or related proceedings.</Note>

## Platform Action

Auto-escalation to human.

### Credit Operations – Next Steps

- Conduct a full review of the applicant’s credit history
- Assess:
  - Whether the bankruptcy has been discharged  
  - Time elapsed since insolvency event  
  - Impact on applicant’s current financial position
- Apply lending policy to determine eligibility or exclusion

## Example Situation

An applicant’s credit report shows a discharged bankruptcy from three years ago.  
The platform escalates the case. Credit ops confirms no ongoing insolvency, reviews current income stability, and proceeds based on lender policy for discharged bankrupts.
